Workflow

6 steps
  1. 1

    Pick a topic

    Choose one problem and one target audience. Write a one-sentence goal.

  2. 2

    Define the outcome

    State what “done” looks like (a deliverable, result, or measurable change).

  3. 3

    Create an outline

    List sections, prerequisites, and the order of actions the reader will take.

  4. 4

    Draft the guide

    Write clear steps, include examples, and keep each step verifiable.

  5. 5

    Review & test

    Follow your own steps end-to-end. Fix gaps, ambiguity, and missing context.

  6. 6

    Publish

    Commit to GitHub and share the GitHub Pages URL.

Tips

  • ✓

    Keep steps atomic

    One action per step; avoid big jumps.

  • ✓

    Show examples

    Include commands, templates, or sample prompts where helpful.

  • ✓

    Call out prerequisites

    Tools, accounts, permissions, and setup.
