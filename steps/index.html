Roadmap Steps

10 steps
  1. 1

    Set Up Your GoHighLevel Account

    30–45 minutesCreate and configure your GoHighLevel agency account, connect Stripe, and set a custom domain.

    Why This Matters: Your platform setup determines how fast you can onboard clients and how professional you look. A clean foundation prevents costly rebuilds later.
    Start
  2. 2

    Understand the AI Agency Business Model

    15–20 minutesLearn where AI creates leverage for local businesses (lead capture, follow‑up, booking, retention) and how agencies charge for outcomes.

    Why This Matters: If you sell “automation,” you compete on price. If you sell outcomes (appointments, revenue, time saved), you become premium and easier to close.
    Start
  3. 3

    Choose Your Target Niche

    20–30 minutesPick a niche where missed calls and slow responses cost money and where your offer can be explained in one sentence.

    Why This Matters: A niche gives you clearer messaging, faster outreach, and repeatable delivery—so you can scale instead of reinventing every client.
    Start
  4. 4

    Find Target Businesses with Google Maps

    45–60 minutesBuild a list of 50+ qualified prospects (name, phone, site, reviews) and note why each is a fit.

    Why This Matters: No pipeline = no agency. A prospect list gives you daily targets and turns growth into a measurable routine.
    Start
  5. 5

    Outreach Strategy & Pricing

    30–40 minutesSet pricing, define your core package, and create simple scripts (DM/email/call) that book appointments.

    Why This Matters: You can’t scale on “custom quotes.” Clear pricing + a repeatable script is what converts attention into paid clients.
    Start
  6. 6

    Maximize Revenue: Websites & AI Ads

    25–35 minutesAdd upsells that increase LTV (smart websites, ads, content/video) without making delivery complicated.

    Why This Matters: Lead capture is valuable, but lead generation multiplies results. Upsells can double revenue per client with minimal extra work.
    Start
  7. 7

    Set Up Client Sub‑Accounts

    20–30 minutes per clientCreate sub‑accounts with branding, phone numbers, calendars, pipelines, permissions, and rebilling.

    Why This Matters: This is your scaling layer. Sub‑accounts keep clients isolated and make onboarding a checklist you can delegate.
    Start
  8. 8

    Build Conversation AI (Chatbots)

    30–45 minutes per clientDeploy a website chatbot that answers FAQs, captures leads, qualifies, and pushes bookings 24/7.

    Why This Matters: Chatbots produce fast visible wins (speed‑to‑lead, more inquiries), which builds trust and retention.
    Start
  9. 9

    Set Up Voice AI (Phone System)

    45–60 minutes per clientConfigure AI phone agents to answer calls, qualify leads, book appointments, and escalate when needed.

    Why This Matters: Every missed call is lost revenue. Voice AI directly protects sales, so clients pay more and stay longer when you report results.
    Start
  10. 10

    Deliver Results & Scale Your Agency

    OngoingTrack KPIs (calls answered, leads captured, appointments booked), report wins, optimize workflows, and build systems to grow to 20+ clients.

    Why This Matters: Retention is the engine of recurring revenue. Reporting outcomes + systemizing delivery creates referrals and predictable growth.
    Start
